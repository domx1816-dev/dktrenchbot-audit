Trading Strategy

Detection Logic

The bot monitors the XRPL ledger for new trustline creations (TrustSet transactions) and evaluates tokens against a multi-factor scoring system:

Factor Threshold Weight
Volume (last 5 min) ≥ 20 XRP Primary filter
Burst TrustSets ≥ 10 in 5 min Primary filter
Quality Score ≥ 30 Entry gate
TVL (Pool Liquidity) ≥ 200 XRP Safety filter

Scoring Components

  • Cluster Detection (+20%): Multiple related wallets creating trustlines simultaneously
  • Alpha Signal (+15%): Early mover advantage from trusted sources
  • ML Prediction (+15%): Historical pattern matching
  • Bull Market Bonus (+10%): Macro sentiment adjustment
  • Smart Wallet Activity (+5% each): Known profitable traders entering

Position Sizing (Dynamic % of Balance)

Tier Score Range Allocation
Elite ≥ 65 20% of balance
Normal ≥ 50 12% of balance
Small ≥ 40 6% of balance
Safety Caps: Maximum 100 XRP per trade | Minimum 3 XRP per trade | Maximum 10 concurrent positions

Exit Strategy

Dynamic Take-Profit Ladder

Milestone Action
2x entry price Sell 50% of position
3x entry price Sell 20% of remaining
5x entry price Sell 15% of remaining
Above 5x 30% trailing stop loss

Stop Loss

  • Hard Stop: -50% from entry price (automatic sell)
  • Trailing Stop: Activates after 5x milestone (30% pullback)

Trustline Cleanup

On every position close, the bot automatically:

  1. Sells dust balances (< 0.000001 XRP equivalent)
  2. Burns remaining token balance (if any)
  3. Removes the trustline to reclaim 0.2 XRP reserve

Risk Management

⚠️ Risk Disclosure: This bot trades highly speculative meme tokens on the XRPL. All capital deployed should be considered at risk. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

Reserve Requirements

  • Base Reserve: 1 XRP (account activation)
  • Trustline Reserve: 0.2 XRP per active trustline
  • Buffer: Bot maintains minimum 10 XRP free balance for operations

Slippage Protection

  • Default Slippage Buffer: 10%
  • Maximum Slippage Check: Rejects trades if expected slippage > 20%

Circuit Breakers

  • Auto-pause on 3 consecutive losses
  • Manual kill switch: kill -9 $(pgrep -f bot.py)
  • Emergency withdrawal to main wallet via RegularKey
